"I need a portfolio site"

Hey, don't worry, I've got you covered!

What's the goal?

I want to provide a quick and easy way to get started showcasing your work online even if you've got basically no money. Instead of trying to get you to give me money, I just want you to showcase your work so I can look at it and go "Ooooo".

First and foremost, this is free of charge, and while some services I may recommend setting up with tutorials may have paid tiers, they never need to be used. The only thing I would ask you would consider buying at any point is a custom domain though Google Domains. I don't get paid to recommend them, I say it because it's the most bang for your buck.

What technologies?

I use Bootstrap and Font Awesome for actual page content, because of course I do. What's Bootstrap for those new to the party? Well, it's an open source toolkit that makes things look not as terrible when you resize a page. Font Awesome, on the other hand, is just a sweet way to add professionally designed icons to things!

For hosting, I use GitHub Pages. If you don't like the idea of fiddling with HTML and just want a theme-maker / don't want to suffer, you can use their built-in one: It's free! In addition, I recommend Google Domains for buying domains since you get email forwarding and it doesn't make me rip my hair out, plus Cloudflare free tier is used for a free SSL certificate.
